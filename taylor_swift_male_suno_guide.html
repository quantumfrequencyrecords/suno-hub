An Antonoff-Style Male Pop Songwriting System for Suno AI
Per-Song Prompts · Universal Formula · Album-by-Album Guides

Each prompt pair below is reverse-engineered from the specific sonic DNA of that song — the instrumentation Antonoff used, the lyrical mode it operates in, the emotional arc, and the structural quirks that made it a hit. All prompts are written for a male vocalist and male perspective, with the Antonoff production signature preserved: Juno-6 synths, reverb-drenched vocals, 80s-influenced drums, layered harmonies, and emotionally raw storytelling.

How to use: Paste the Style prompt into Suno's Style field → paste the Lyric Generation prompt into the Lyrics field → click "Generate Lyrics" → review and hit Create. Regenerate lyrics as many times as you want for a unique song each time.

Emotional core: leaving the past behind, the city as metaphor for reinvention. Lyrics are broad, cinematic, almost manifesto-like. Repeating hook phrase drives the chorus. Male version: a man arriving in a new city or chapter of life, shedding an old identity.

Lyric Generation Prompt → Lyrics Field Click Generate Lyrics
Write an anthem for a man who has just moved to a new city and is shedding his entire old identity — old name, old reputation, old heartbreaks. The tone is euphoric and almost manifesto-like, not reflective. The city itself becomes a character: it doesn't know his past, it offers him a blank slate. The chorus should be a huge repeated hook using the city (or a metaphor for it) as the central image. Lyrics should feel broad and cinematic rather than specific — more declaration than story. Structure: short punchy verses building momentum → massive anthem chorus with layered imagery → second verse with a single new detail → same chorus → brief bridge about what he's leaving behind → final exploding chorus. Under 3:30. The very first line should land like stepping off a train.

Write a male-perspective synth-pop song in the style of Antonoff-produced, confessionally honest 2014–2022 pop. Step 1 — Go to Suno
Open suno.com → click Create → switch to Custom Mode.

Step 2 — Paste the Style Prompt
Copy the Style prompt for the song or album you want → paste into the Style field. This controls instrumentation, tempo, production texture, and mood.

Step 3 — Paste the Lyric Generation Prompt
Copy the Lyric Generation Prompt → paste it into the Lyrics field → click "Generate Lyrics." Suno will write the actual song lyrics based on your brief. You don't write the lyrics — Suno does.

Step 4 — Review and Regenerate
Read the generated lyrics. If they don't feel right, click Generate Lyrics again — each regeneration produces a completely different set of lyrics from the same brief. You can regenerate as many times as you like.

Step 5 — Create
Once you're happy with the lyrics, click Create. Suno will generate two audio versions. Try both.

Pro Tips
— The style prompt controls the SOUND. The lyric prompt controls the STORY. They work independently.
— You can mix and match: take the Cruel Summer style prompt but use the London Boy lyric brief.
— Regenerate lyrics 3–5 times before choosing — later generations often get more specific and interesting.
— No artist names in either field — Suno flags them. Use genre and era descriptors only.
— After generating audio, use Suno's "Extend" feature to add an outro or bridge if needed.
